Questions buyers will ask

Answer the objections clearly and close the gap between interest and download.

Does it work only in one app?

No. WASPrFlow is built to insert text into any Windows app where you can type, including browsers, Office apps, email clients, chat apps, and IDEs.

Is this just speech-to-text?

No. The difference is that WASPrFlow focuses on ready-to-use writing. It transcribes quickly, then cleans up the text before inserting it into your current field.

What if I do not want AI rewrite on some dictations?

Use Privacy Mode. That disables the OpenAI rewrite path so your text is only transcribed through Deepgram.

Do I need an account?

No. WASPrFlow does not require account creation or a persistent online login to use the product.

Which installer should I pick?

The Setup EXE is best for most individual users. The MSI is better for managed or IT-administered Windows environments.
