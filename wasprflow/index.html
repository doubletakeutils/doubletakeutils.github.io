FAQ

Which installer should I download?

Use the Setup EXE for most users. Use the MSI if your organization deploys software with managed tools.

How do I activate my license?

After purchase, use the same email in WASPrFlow. You can also paste your key or import your .lic file from the License panel.

What if my key does not activate?

Check that you used the purchase email, then try importing your saved .lic file. If it still fails, reply to your purchase email for support.
